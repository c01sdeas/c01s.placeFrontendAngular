At c01s.place, we use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, provide our services, and ensure the proper functioning of our platform. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your options for managing your cookie preferences.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files placed on your device to collect standard internet log information and visitor behavior information. They help us remember your preferences and improve your experience on our website.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies on c01s.place:

2.1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the operation of our platform. They allow you to navigate our site, access secure areas, and use our services. Without these cookies, some parts of our platform would not function.

2.2. Preference Cookies

These cookies enable us to remember your preferences (such as language and region) and customize your experience on the platform. They help us personalize your settings.

2.3. Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how users interact with our platform. This data helps us improve the user experience, enhance platform features, and monitor performance. The data we collect may include:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited
  • Referring URLs
  • Time spent on pages
  • User actions (clicks, shares, searches)

This data is typically anonymized and aggregated.

2.4. Advertising Cookies (Third-Party)

We may use third-party advertising cookies, such as Google AdSense, to display targeted advertisements to users. These cookies collect data to serve ads relevant to your interests. Third-party advertisers may use your browsing behavior and other information to personalize ads.

For more information about how Google uses advertising cookies, visit the Google Privacy Policy.

3. How to Manage Cookies

You have the right to accept or refuse cookies. You can set your browser to refuse all cookies or to notify you when a cookie is being sent. However, please note that if you do not accept cookies, some parts of our platform may not function properly.

  • You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies or delete them after they have been set.
  • You can also opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings and About Ads.

4. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes, including:

  • To ensure the proper functioning of the platform
  • To personalize your user experience
  • To analyze platform usage and improve our services
  • To provide relevant advertisements and content
  • For security and fraud prevention

5. Sharing of Data Collected Through Cookies

We do not share your personal information collected through cookies with third parties unless stated in our Privacy Policy. We may share aggregated, anonymized data with our advertising partners to provide targeted ads.

6. Your Rights Regarding Cookies

As per your privacy rights outlined in our Privacy Policy, you have the right to manage, update, and delete the cookies we use by adjusting your browser settings or through any consent tools we provide. If you wish to exercise your right to object or withdraw consent, you can do so by contacting us through the details in our Privacy Policy.
